What is Pushpa?

Pushpa – The Mafia Game is an online, chat-based adaptation of the classic Social Deduction game Mafia, enhanced with a Pushpa – themed storyline. Each player is secretly assigned a role aligned with either the Syndicate or Pushpa. Pushpa smuggles Sandalwood and eliminates one Syndicate member per night, and act innocent during the day. Others must combine their wits and eliminate the real Pushpa before they're outnumbered!
